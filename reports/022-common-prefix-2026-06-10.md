   - Adjacent experience: 4 years of EVM ecosystem partnerships (cv.md line 37); fluency in market structure (line 20).

Before applying, spend 1-2 days on Common Prefix's published research, their Bridges work, and chaingrowth.xyz so the first call shows genuine product understanding.
2. **Network skew: NFT/token projects vs. L1s/L2s/CEXs/DEXs/asset issuers.**
   - Not a hard blocker — there IS overlap (token projects = asset issuers; NFT projects touch L2s).
   - Mitigation: in outreach, name the categories where the network is real (token founders, ecosystem leads) and be honest about where it's thinner (market makers, CEX listings desks aside from his exchange-facing contacts). Frame: "network compounds — I built a 40-project Rolodex in 4 years from zero."
3. **No employment history at an infra company; founder-side only.**
   - Mitigation: use the exit narrative (modes/_profile.md): owner-operator who has only ever done full-accountability work. For a sole-BD-hire role, founder experience is a feature, not a bug — no playbook will be handed to him.
4. **Research-heavy culture (peer-reviewed publications, "world-class scientists").**
   - Mitigation: position as the commercial complement to a scientific team — the person who translates research strength into signed partnerships. Don't fake technical depth; demonstrate curiosity.
