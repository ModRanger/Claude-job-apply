Per profile policy (walk-away below $60K base unless commission structure is exceptional AND documented) — nothing is documented here. Roles written like this ("we care about hustle, not resumes," anonymous team) are frequently commission/token-only or trial-based.
4. **Anonymous company.** No website, no entity name, no founders, no funding trail found. For a role that would have Viet vouching for the product to his own network, attaching his reputation to an unverifiable platform is itself a cost.
