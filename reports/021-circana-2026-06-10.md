### Gaps & Mitigation

1. **"5+ years providing administrative support" + AI-assisted screening (HARD GAP, compounded).** The JD states: "This role is subject to AI-assisted screening… helping locate details in resumes that relate to the job description." An AI screener matching the resume against "administrative support," "executive," "Concur," "DocuSign," "PowerPoint" will find almost none of those tokens in Viet's CV. Mitigation would require a near-total CV rewrite in admin vocabulary — and even then, the experience is owner-side, not support-side. This is the single highest screen-out probability in the batch.
2. **Enterprise tool stack (DocuSign, Adobe, Concur, PowerPoint).** All learnable, none evidenced. Mitigation: only DocuSign/e-sign and spreadsheet claims that are actually true; do not keyword-stuff tools he hasn't used.
3. **No corporate environment experience.** The role lives in enterprise process (sponsorship coordination with trade associations, distribution lists, minutes). Mitigation: none strong; the salon/NFT background reads as a different world here.
